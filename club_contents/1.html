University life at Sunway is more than just academics — it’s a journey of self-discovery, leadership, and meaningful connections. At the heart of this vibrant student ecosystem is the Sunway University Student Council (SUSC) — a dynamic body dedicated to championing student voices, driving impactful initiatives, and shaping a well-rounded university experience.

SUSC plays a pivotal role in steering over 100 student-led clubs and societies, each offering unique opportunities to explore interests, build networks, and contribute to causes that matter. These include globally and nationally affiliated NGOs such as the Rotaract Club of Sunway University, TEDxSunway University, Sunway Toastmasters Club, and Sunway Model United Nations (MUN) - just to name a few.

Beyond campus, SUSC ensures that Sunway University has a strong presence on the national stage. The Council, working closely with Student LIFE, actively engages in key platforms and collaborates with national bodies such as:

  • Malaysian Association of Private Colleges and Universities (MAPCU)
  • MASISWA and Majlis Sukan IPTS (Private Institution Sports Council)
  • Sekretariat Rukun Negara
  • Private Higher Education Institution Student Council (ISC)
  • Majlis HEP-IPTS (Council of Student Affairs for Private Higher Learning Institutions)

Together, we cultivate leadership, celebrate diversity, and inspire change — within our university and far beyond its walls.
