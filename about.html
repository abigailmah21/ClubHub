ClubHub is a student-centered platform developed to streamline and enhance the way students engage with campus life at Sunway University and Sunway College. Our purpose is to centralize all student organizations, events, and communications in one accessible, user-friendly hub.

With over 100 clubs and societies spanning various categories — including academic, cultural, business, arts, sports, volunteering, and more — we aim to foster a vibrant, inclusive, and empowered student community. Whether you're a student looking to explore interests, a club leader managing members, or a university admin overseeing student activities, ClubHub offers the tools and structure to support your goals.

We are committed to supporting student development, leadership, and engagement. Through ClubHub, we strive to create a space that encourages collaboration, celebrates diversity, and inspires students to contribute meaningfully to campus and society.

At the heart of ClubHub is the belief that every student has something valuable to contribute — and that every interest, voice, and initiative deserves a platform.
